WELCOME TO GOPAL KRUSHNA GOKHALE COLLEGE KOLHAPUR

The College was established by the Shikshan Prasarak Mandal on 20th June 1950. The Mandal was the creation of two distinguished\n men of eminence - Principal M.R. Desai and Barrister Balasaheb Khardekar, whose dream it was to establish a centre of higher learning in western\n Maharashtra, along the lines of 'Shanti Niketan' in Bengal. Thus the College was originally located in Kagal, a village near Kolhapur; later it was\n shifted from Kagal to Kolhapur in June 1954. In the beginning, the College had courses in the Arts stream only, but later the Science and Commerce\n streams were added. Interestingly, this College which began with seven teachers and just one student in 1950 has over the years grown into a great\n center of higher education catering to the requirements of the students and parents of this part of the country. The professed mission of the College\n is 'to impart education to those who have been kept out of the purview of education, to elevate their intellectual and overall personality'.

Affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur, Gopal Krishna Gokhale College secured the recognition of the University Grants\n Commission under 2 (F) and under 12 (B) in December, 1987.The College is housed in a well-constructed and well-maintained building in a campus\n area of 1.12 acres within the city of Kolhapur. Its location ensures easy access, and the easy availability of public transport facilities is of great\n help to the students. There is another site of around 10 acres a few kilometers away, where the College auditorium is established and where the students\n are provided playground facilities."

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:Aspirants seeking admission to 3 year Full-time BCA Program should have passed in 10+2 examination in relevant stream or possess an equivalent qualification from recognized board.
